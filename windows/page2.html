OUR SERVICES

DOMESTIC WINDOW CLEANING Our Residential or Domestic Window Cleaners will have your house or apartment windows shining in a short time! It is often only after your windows are cleaned that you notice how dirty they were. You will enjoy the view and added sense of space, and it will give your home a nice and fresh look. It makes sense because windows let light in which illuminates the entire house!

STRATA WINDOW CLEANING Strata Managers or representatives can contact us to discuss their needs and we will offer the options available on how to best handle cleaning all the common area and difficult access windows in the block. Access to the windows is most economical when individual apartments can be reached from inside. This offers a more personal service and the tenants can choose to individually have the rest of their windows cleaned at the same time. We can also use waterfed poles externally for windows up to three floors high.

And we are also experienced, qualified, licensed and insured to use a Cherry Picker, which is perfect for strata blocks of apartments of up to five storeys high. An advantage of using these Elevating Work Platforms is that we get up close to every window and will deliver an excellent clean, the same quality as if your windows were on the ground floor.

COMMERCIAL MONTHLY WINDOW CLEANING Available for cafes, restaurants and gyms, which usually need this service monthly to improve hygiene and to make your business more attractive to your customers.

POST CONSTRUCTION WINDOW CLEANING Builders and Owner-Builders can call us to arrange our highly specialised builder's window cleaning of new or renovated houses, apartments and business premises.

REAL ESTATE WINDOW CLEANING Real Estate Agents can easily arrange to have our Window Cleaning service at end of tenancy, which will set the standard which the incoming tenant will need to reach when they eventually vacate house or apartment. Sparkling windows enhance a property and its surrounding views, and will undoubtedly help sell properties on the market.
