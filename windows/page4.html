WHY CLEAN WINDOWS? Regular window cleaning provides cleaner air to breathe and the luxury of a brighter more positive environment for you and all the other people who use your space, like your family and friends, or your colleagues and your clients. Maintaining all surfaces including glass surfaces clean and free from germs is important to staying healthy. Glass panels on windows, sliding doors and mirrors represent a large percentage of the surface areas of indoor spaces, especially in modern homes and businesses with large windows and floor to ceiling glass panels. If there is any degree of visible dirt, or stuffy or mild smells, you can refresh your home or work space with a professional window cleaning service.
